# Job-offer readiness

**Purpose:** Staff determination of who gets actively promoted to hiring partners. Separate from pass/fail — pass is required but not sufficient for maximum placement effort.

---

## Readiness checklist

Student must **pass** plus demonstrate the three program abilities with evidence:

### 1. Review others' work

| Evidence | Threshold |
|----------|-----------|
| Reviews submitted | 87/87 (29 × 3) |
| Review quality (spot-check) | Median ≥ 3/5 if sampled |
| Platform PR reviews (Phase 1 post-cutover) | ≥ 9 total |

### 2. Produce work others approve

| Evidence | Threshold (any 2 of 4) |
|----------|--------------------------|
| Won a Phase 1 vote | 1 win |
| Learning app users | ≥ 50 (2× pass bar) |
| Venture investor touch | ≥ 2 qualified |
| Open source | ≥ 2 merged PRs |

### 3. Contribute at high level to others' codebases

| Evidence | Threshold |
|----------|-----------|
| Merged platform PRs | ≥ 1 merged to pm/comms/showcase |
| OR merged OSS PR | ≥ 1 (same as pass) |
| OR substantial open OSS PR | CI green + ≥ 3 maintainer comments (pass substitute) |

---

## Process

| Step | When | Owner |
|------|------|-------|
| Auto-compute checklist from metrics | Aug 18 | Script + program director |
| Director review borderline cases | Aug 18 | Program director |
| Student self-nomination optional | Aug 18 | Student adds 200-word "ready statement" on showcase |
| Readiness list published | Aug 19 pre-showcase | Placement lead |
| Partner packet sent | Aug 20 | Placement lead |

**Borderline:** Pass but misses 1 readiness item → "developing" tier; still get intros, lower featured priority.

---

## Putting forward

| Tier | Partner treatment |
|------|-------------------|
| **Ready** (meets checklist) | Featured on homepage; priority intro queue; placement lead pre-briefs partners |
| **Pass** (not ready) | Listed in directory; intros on request; no featured placement |
| **Developing** | Pass + close on readiness; 1 targeted intro if partner fit |

All passers attend showcase. Ready students get lightning demo slots 1–20 (random among ready if > 20).

---

## Feedback loop

When partner passes on student after intro:

1. Placement lead logs reason (skills gap, role mismatch, comp)
2. Student receives written feedback within 5 days
3. If ≥ 3 passes with same gap → suggested focus for re-enrollment
4. Re-enrollment plan optional 15-min call with placement lead

Does not void guarantee.

---

## Guarantee handoff

Readiness list → [placement-referrals.md](../business/placement-referrals.md):

- Active placement effort for **all passers** for 180 days
- **Ready** tier gets weekly partner outreach including student's profile
- Offer verification against [guarantee-refunds-buyouts.md](../business/guarantee-refunds-buyouts.md)
