# Business model

One-time tuition, unlimited cohort enrollment until placed, revenue concentrated in employer referral fees with a 10% student kickback. Marginal cost per student near zero; larger cohorts are better.

## Components

- [pricing.md](pricing.md) — tuition and student-borne costs
- [guarantee-refunds-buyouts.md](guarantee-refunds-buyouts.md) — the job guarantee, refunds, retakes, buyouts
- [placement-referrals.md](placement-referrals.md) — referral fees and the student kickback
- [subsidies.md](subsidies.md) — future subsidies once revenue-positive
- [financial-model.md](financial-model.md) — the numbers end to end

## The worked example (decided)

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Student pays tuition | $10,000 |
| Student hired at | $200,000 salary |
| Program collects referral fee (~25%) | $50,000 |
| Student receives 10% kickback | $5,000 |
| **Student's net cost** | **$5,000** |
