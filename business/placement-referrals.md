# Placement and referral fees

**Purpose:** Operations from showcase through hire — agreements, kickbacks, self-sourced offers, matching.

---

## Standard partner agreement (summary)

Full template: General Counsel. Terms locked for cohort 1:

| Term | Value |
|------|-------|
| Fee | **25%** of first-year base salary |
| Due | Net 30 from employee start date |
| Clawback | **100%** if termination ≤ 90 days |
| Non-circumvention | **12 months** from intro |
| Exclusivity | None |

---

## Kickback mechanics

| Step | Timing |
|------|--------|
| Employer pays referral fee | Per agreement |
| Program retains 90% | — |
| Student receives **10%** | Within **30 days** of fee collection |
| Tax | Student receives 1099 (US) — student responsible |

**Clawback:** If fee clawed back within 90 days, student returns kickback (pro-rated if partial clawback).

Example: $50k fee → $5k kickback to student; program net $45k.

---

## Self-sourced and non-partner offers

| Source | Guarantee fulfilled? | Referral fee? |
|--------|---------------------|---------------|
| Partner from pipeline | Yes | Yes |
| Partner introduced but hired later | Yes (non-circumvention) | Yes |
| Cold application (no prior intro) | **Yes** | No |
| Student's own network, never introduced | **Yes** | No |

Student submits offer letter → placement lead verifies qualifying criteria → guarantee closed → no kickback unless partner fee applies.

## Placement operations

| Function | Owner | Activity |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Partner matching | Placement lead | Weekly partner blasts with ready-tier profiles |
| Intro routing | Placement lead | 24 hr SLA on request intros |

| Offer negotiation | Student; placement lead advisory | Comp benchmarking on request |
| Offer verification | Placement lead | Letter + start date + role title |
| Invoice | Finance | Net 30 from start |
