## Tuition

|------|--------|

### Competitor context (validation)

| Program | Price | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Gauntlet | ~$0 upfront + placement | Different model |
| Boot.dev / similar | $30–50/mo | No guarantee |
| Lambda / legacy bootcamps | $15–30k | ISA variants |
| Hult MBA (context) | $50k+/yr | Different product |

$10k one-time with job guarantee + unlimited terms is **aggressive but defensible** given referral revenue model.
