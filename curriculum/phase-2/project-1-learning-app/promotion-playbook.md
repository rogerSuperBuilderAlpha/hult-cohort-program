# Learning app: promotion playbook

**Purpose:** Students promote learning apps to hit ≥ 25 external users by Fri week 6. Distribution is graded — this is the curriculum.

---

## Promotion timeline (week 6 — one week)

| Day | Focus |
|-----|-------|
| **Tue** | Integrate + soft launch to 5 friends for QA |
| **Wed–Thu** | Public launch; primary promotion push |
| **Fri** | Optimize funnel; snapshot at 17:00 ET |

---

## Channels (prioritized)

| Channel | Action | Expected users |
|---------|--------|----------------|
| **Platform directory** | Optimize title, description, icon | 5–10 |
| **Personal network** | LinkedIn post + DMs to 20 relevant contacts | 5–10 |
| **Communities** | 1 post each in 2 relevant subreddits/Discords (follow rules) | 5–15 |
| **Showcase profile** | Link app from cohort showcase | 2–5 |
| **Twitter/X thread** | Demo video 60 sec | 3–10 |

---

## Rules of engagement

| Allowed | Not allowed |
|---------|-------------|
| Posting in relevant communities | Spamming unrelated forums |
| Asking friends to try honestly | Cohort members registering as users |
| Paid ads ≤ $50 total | Bot signups, incentive farms |
| Cross-promotion with classmates (different niches) | Fake reviews |

Violations → metric zeroed for that app + academic integrity review.

---

## Analytics setup

- Primary: Ludwitt/Hult platform dashboard (official metric)
- Secondary: Plausible/Umami on your app for funnel debugging (not used for pass/fail)

Track: listing views → launches → lesson_started events.

---

## Launch checklist (Wednesday week 6)

- [ ] App approved and listed in directory
- [ ] Launch post drafted (LinkedIn + 1 community)
- [ ] Demo video recorded (≤ 90 sec)
- [ ] Showcase profile updated
- [ ] Support channel (email or GitHub issues) live
