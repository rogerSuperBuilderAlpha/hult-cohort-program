## Investor touchpoint (pass gate)

≥ **1 qualified touchpoint** logged in `INVESTOR_LOG.md`:

```markdown
## 2026-08-18 — Jane Doe — Angel at XYZ Capital
- Type: 30-min video call
- Outcome: Requested follow-up Q1; no term sheet
- Verified by: placement lead ✓
```

Qualifies: angel, VC, corp dev, family office. Does not qualify: friends, cohort peers, program staff.
