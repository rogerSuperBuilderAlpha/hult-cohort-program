# Venture: market research packet

**Due:** Fri Dec 5 (draft); final Dec 12.

---

## Required contents

| Section | Minimum |
|---------|---------|
| **Problem** | 1 page — who hurts, how much, evidence |
| **Customer discovery** | **5 interviews** summarized (quotes + names/roles or anonymized) |
| **Market size** | TAM/SAM/SOM with sources cited |
| **Competition** | ≥ 5 competitors; positioning matrix |
| **Target customer** | ICP: role, company size, geography |
| **Wedge** | Why you win vs incumbents |

---

## Evidence standards

| Acceptable | Not acceptable |
|------------|----------------|
| Recorded or notes from real calls | Pure desk research only |
| Surveys with ≥ 20 responses | ChatGPT-generated "interviews" |
| Public data (Statista, SEC, etc.) | Unsourced TAM numbers |

Research agent may draft synthesis; **human must conduct interviews.**

# Market research: {Company name}
