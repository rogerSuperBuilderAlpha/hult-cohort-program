# Hiring Partner Referral Agreement (DRAFT)

⚠️ **DRAFT FOR GENERAL COUNSEL REVIEW ONLY**

Between Hult International University / [Program Entity] ("Hult") and _________________ ("Partner").

## 1. Services

Hult provides Partner access to cohort graduate profiles and introduction facilitation. Partner conducts independent hiring evaluation.

## 2. Referral fee

When Partner hires a graduate Hult introduced or who Partner first encountered through Hult showcase/cohort materials ("Candidate"):

| Term | Value |
|------|-------|
| Fee | **25%** of Candidate's first-year base salary |
| Excludes | Signing bonus unless > 20% of total first-year comp |
| Due | Net 30 from Candidate start date |
| Invoice | Hult invoices Partner on start date |

## 3. Clawback

Partner receives **100% credit** against future fees if Candidate terminates or is terminated for cause within **90 days** of start. Hult returns fee within 30 days of notice.

## 4. Non-circumvention

Fee applies to any Candidate Partner hires within **12 months** of introduction, even if hire is delayed or Candidate applied directly after introduction.

## 5. No exclusivity

Partner has no exclusive access to graduates. Graduates may accept multiple offers.

## 6. EEO

Partner hires in compliance with applicable employment discrimination laws. Hult does not control hiring decisions.

## 7. Data

Partner receives public portfolio data only. Partner protects Candidate data per applicable privacy law.

## 8. Term

12 months from signature; auto-renews unless 30-day notice.
