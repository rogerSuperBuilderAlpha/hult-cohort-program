# Program Agreement (DRAFT)

⚠️ **DRAFT FOR GENERAL COUNSEL REVIEW ONLY — NOT FOR SIGNATURE**

Between Hult International University ("Hult") and the student named below ("Student").

---

## 1. Program

Student enrolls in the **Hult Cohort Developer Program**, a non-credit continuing-education certificate program consisting of one or more cohort terms until a Qualifying Job Offer (defined below) or voluntary withdrawal.

## 2. Tuition

| Term | Amount |
|------|--------|
| One-time program fee | **$10,000 USD** |
| Due | First day of Student's first cohort term (Week 1) |
| Covers | Unlimited cohort term re-enrollments until Qualifying Job Offer |

Tooling subscriptions (Cursor, Claude Code) are paid directly by Student to vendors (~$400/month).

## 3. Job placement commitment

Hult commits to active placement assistance for Students who **Pass** a cohort term (per program pass/fail criteria). Hult does **not** guarantee employment at a specific employer, salary, or timeline beyond the re-enrollment remedy in Section 4.

Upon Student's acceptance of a **Qualifying Job Offer**, Student's enrollment obligation under this Agreement is fulfilled.

### Qualifying Job Offer

A written offer of **full-time employment** (≥ 32 hours/week) in a **software development, engineering, DevOps, or closely related technical role** with **base salary ≥ $80,000 USD** (or published local equivalent), with start date within **180 days** of Student's Pass date, for a permanent position or contract ≥ 12 months.

Self-sourced and partner-sourced offers qualify equally.

## 4. Re-enrollment (guarantee remedy)

If Student Passes but does not receive a Qualifying Job Offer within 180 days, or fails to Pass, Student may re-enroll in a subsequent cohort term at **no additional tuition** until Student receives a Qualifying Job Offer.

Re-enrollment requires term registration and continued payment of tooling subscriptions.

## 5. Refunds

| Withdrawal timing | Refund |
|-------------------|--------|
| Before first cohort term start | 100% of tuition |
| During Week 1 (by Friday 5pm local cohort time) | 100% of tuition |
| After Week 1 | No cash refund; re-enrollment rights per Section 4 |

## 6. Student obligations

Student must participate in mandatory checkpoints (≤ 20% missed without accommodation), complete all project units, and cooperate with placement process. Failure voids Sections 3–4 placement commitment (re-enrollment may still be offered at Hult's discretion).

## 7. Referral kickback

If Student is hired through a Hult hiring partner and Hult collects a referral fee, Hult pays Student **10% of the fee collected** within 30 days of receipt. Student responsible for applicable taxes.

## 8. Buyout

Hult may offer Student a one-time payment (default $5,000) in exchange for signed Release and Separation Agreement, terminating all rights under this Agreement. Hult-initiated only.

## 9. Public work and IP

Student consents to public display of project work on GitHub and cohort showcase. Student retains ownership of Student-created code (MIT license in cohort template repos). See Expectations Acknowledgment.

## 10. Dispute resolution

Binding arbitration in [jurisdiction — GC to specify]. Prevailing party attorneys' fees as permitted by law.

## 11. Entire agreement

This Agreement supersedes marketing materials except the published pass/fail criteria and Qualifying Job Offer definition incorporated by reference.
