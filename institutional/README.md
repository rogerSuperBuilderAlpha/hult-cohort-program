# Institutional (Hult)

Fitting the program inside Hult International University: policy compatibility, legal exposure, and the structural relationship.

## Components

- [policy-review.md](policy-review.md) — enrollment, credit, refund policy compatibility
- [legal-risk.md](legal-risk.md) — guarantee liability, public student work, buyouts

## Why this is the highest-stakes unknown

The program's most distinctive features — pass/fail, money-back guarantee, unlimited repeat enrollment, pay-to-leave buyouts, job guarantees — are all unusual for an accredited university. Whether they survive policy and legal review intact determines how much of the model ships as designed.
