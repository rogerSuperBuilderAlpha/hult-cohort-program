## Guarantee contract language

Program Agreement must include (from [guarantee-refunds-buyouts.md](../business/guarantee-refunds-buyouts.md)):

- Qualifying offer definition ($80k+, full-time SWE, 180 days)
- Student obligation void conditions
- Unlimited re-enrollment (not cash payout on failure to place)
- Arbitration clause

⚠️ **DECISION NEEDED (General Counsel):** Draft and approve before marketing.

## Buyout agreements

Release and Separation Agreement includes:

- Mutual release of claims
- Mutual non-disparagement (12 months)
- Confidentiality of buyout amount
- Forfeiture of re-enrollment and guarantee rights
- No admission of wrongdoing

Template: ⚠️ General Counsel.

## Public student work

### Enrollment consent (Expectations Acknowledgment)

Students consent at enrollment to:

- Public GitHub repos in cohort org
- Public showcase profile (opt-out available — private profile, cannot win showcase operator)
- Hiring partner access to portfolio
- Recording of lightning demo at showcase

### IP ownership

| Work | Owner |
|------|-------|
| Student build repos | **Student** (MIT license in template) |
| Winning platform repos | **Operator + team** during term; contributors retain copyright on commits |
| Ludwitt/Hult integrated apps | **Student**; platform gets license to host |

Students may remove showcase profile on request post-graduation (30-day takedown).

## Data on student-operated platforms

Cohort comms may run on peer-built software — not enterprise SaaS.

| Expectation | Policy |
|-------------|--------|
| DM privacy | Operators may not read DMs ([operator-handbook](../curriculum/phase-1/project-2-comms-platform/operator-handbook.md)) |
| Data retention | Operators must export cohort data on request at term end |
| Breach | Operator notifies program director within 24 hr; director notifies cohort |
| Passwords | Hashed; no plaintext storage |

Incident playbook: director emails cohort → rotate credentials → post-mortem in 72 hr.

## Employment law: referral kickback

10% student kickback on referral fee:

- US: structured as **bonus from program**, not employer payment — 1099 from program
- UK/EU: ⚠️ Finance review per jurisdiction before cohort 2 international expansion

Not tied to employee performance — one-time on fee collection.
