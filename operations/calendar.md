## Weekly rhythm

All times in **cohort campus local timezone**. Online students join the timezone of their enrolled campus cohort.

| Day | Event | Duration | Attendance |
|-----|-------|----------|------------|
| **Tuesday 10:00** | Kickoff / lecture (when applicable) | 60 min | Required |
| **Tuesday 11:00** | Office hours (program staff) | 60 min | Optional |
| **Wednesday** | Async build day | — | — |
| **Thursday 10:00** | Demo prep / peer Q&A | 45 min | Required during build weeks |
| **Friday 14:00** | Review submissions due (Phase 1) | — | Required during review weeks |
| **Friday 16:00** | Vote closes (Phase 1) | — | Required during vote weeks |
| **Friday 16:30** | Week retro (async post in cohort comms) | 15 min | Required |

**Reasoning:** Tuesday kickoffs give Mon for prep; Friday reviews/votes create a weekly cadence; Wed–Thu are deep work. Matches how distributed teams ship.

## Holidays and breaks

| Break | Handling |
|-------|----------|
| **Thanksgiving week (US, week 12)** | No live Tuesday/Thursday sessions. All deadlines remain. Staff office hours cancelled. |
| **UK/EU local holidays** | Online/async substitution; no deadline extensions unless campus closed entire week (rare) |
| **Religious observances** | Individual accommodations via program director; checkpoint deadlines may shift ±48 hrs |

Program does **not** pause for Thanksgiving. Venture research is async-friendly that week.

## Buffer policy

| Item | Hard (no extension) | Soft (program director may grant +48 hrs) |

| Review submissions | ✅ | — |
| Votes | ✅ | — |
| Build deploy URL for review | ✅ | — |
| Winner cutover complete | ✅ | — |
| Ecosystem unification demo | ✅ | — |
| Team selection picks submitted | ✅ | — |
| Learning app integration | — | ✅ (once, +48 hrs) |
| Venture deck quality | — | ✅ (draft acceptable; final by week 15) |
| Open source first PR opened | — | ✅ |
| Showcase RSVP prep | — | ✅ |

**Reasoning:** Phase 1 hard deadlines train the core skill — others depend on your reviews and votes. Phase 2 external deadlines are softer because the market doesn't extend deadlines either, but agent leverage makes +48 hr recovery reasonable once per project.

### If a project overruns

1. **Build not deployed by review deadline:** Build is ineligible for votes; student may still review others (counts toward pass); may re-enroll next cohort to compete.
2. **Reviews incomplete:** Automatic fail for that project unit; may re-enroll.
3. **Vote tie:** Instant runoff using ranked-choice ballots (see [governance/winner-selection.md](../governance/winner-selection.md) — Tier 2, but calendar assumes ranked choice).
4. **Winner can't cutover in 48 hrs:** Interim operator = runner-up by vote count; original winner has 1 week to finish or loses operator role.
