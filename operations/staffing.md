# Staffing

## Cohort 1 roles

| Role | FTE | Loaded cost (6 mo) | Person |
|------|-----|-------------------|--------|
| **Program director** | 0.5 | $22,500 | Curriculum, kickoffs, votes, pass/fail, disputes |
| **Placement lead** | 1.0 | $37,500 | Partner pipeline, showcase, intros, offers |
| **Admissions (contract)** | 0.25 | $15,000 | Applications, take-home grading |
| **Founder (partner relations)** | 0.1 | Sweat / allocated $10k | First 5 partners |

**Total cohort 1 staff cost:** ~$109,000 (incl. events, legal, marketing from financial model).

Partner relations merges into placement lead cohort 2+.

## Mentorship

**Deliberately minimal.** Peers + agents are the curriculum.

| Available | Not provided |
|-----------|--------------|
| 30-min venture office hour (placement lead) | Dedicated engineering mentor |
| Optional peer swap on business plan | Staff code review |
| Agent configs in week 1 | Lecture-heavy instruction |

Expert guests: **2 per semester** (optional, founder network) — 60-min Q&A, no grading.
